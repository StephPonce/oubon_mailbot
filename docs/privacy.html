Privacy Policy

Effective:

This Privacy Policy describes how Oubon Shop (“Oubon,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our websites, Shopify integrations, AI assistants, product-research tools, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, store URL, business details.
  • Store & Order Data (via Shopify): product listings, inventory, orders, customers’ contact/shipping info as needed to provide the Services. Access is limited to the scopes you authorize.
  • Communications: messages you send to our support email, in-app chat, or via connected inboxes (e.g., Gmail).
  • Usage & Device: logs, IP address, timestamps, and basic device/browser info.
  • Research Signals (optional sources you connect): trend and sentiment inputs from platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, X/Twitter, Reddit, AliExpress/Alibaba, Google Trends, and other supplier catalogs.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide & improve the Services and operate our Shopify integrations.
  • Product Research: analyze market signals (e.g., TikTok/IG trends, supplier catalogs, reviews, pricing, and sentiment) to recommend products and content for your store.
  • AI-Assisted Workflows: draft product descriptions, summarize emails, propose replies, or schedule posts/ad drafts. Automated actions that could change your storefront require your approval unless you explicitly enable auto-publish.
  • Communications: respond to support requests and send important notices.
  • Security & Compliance: detect abuse, debug issues, and meet legal obligations.

3) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Performance of a contract, legitimate interests (e.g., securing and improving the Services), and consent where required.

4) Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: hosting, analytics, error monitoring, AI inference (e.g., model providers), email and notification services, each bound by confidentiality.
  • Integrated Platforms: Shopify, TikTok, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), X/Twitter, Reddit, AliExpress/Alibaba, Google APIs—limited to what’s necessary to deliver the features you enable.
  • Legal/Compliance: to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale with appropriate protections.

5) Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal/operational constraints) at any time.

6) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7) International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

8) Your Rights

  • EEA/UK: access, correct, delete, restrict, object, and data portability. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
  • California: access, delete, correct, opt-out of certain sharing; we do not knowingly sell personal information.

9) Children

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 (or as defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect their data.

10) Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version with an updated “Effective” date.
